About A.P.j Abdul kalam


Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was an iconic Indian aerospace scientist, author, and the 11th President of India (2002-2007), revered as the "Missile Man" for his pivotal role in India's missile and nuclear programs, and as the "People's President" for his humility and focus on youth empowerment and making India a developed nation by 2020.
